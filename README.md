# Music Analytics Project -
I was asked to analyze a 2026 music streaming habits dataset containing listener behavior across different countries, platforms, subscription types, genres, moods, and engagement metrics. The goal was to uncover how users listen to music, what drives engagement, and what patterns could help a music streaming business improve retention, subscriptions, and user experience. 
My objective was to identify listening patterns, engagement trends, and user segments that could help the business answer questions such as:
Which users are most engaged?
What behaviors are associated with paid subscriptions?
Which features appear to keep people listening longer?
Are there signs that certain user groups are at risk of churn, such as high skip rates or low playlist activity?
